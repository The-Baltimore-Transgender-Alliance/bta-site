UNIFY

We must come together in Unity

...if we want to that we can heal together collectively. We must bridge our divides of ability, race, class, language, and identity such that we can truly build our communities with comprehensive solidarity. Our diversity makes us powerful.

RECLAIM

We must Reclaim our stories

...and overwhelm the mainstream with accounts of trans experience, beauty, and power. --writing, speaking, and shouting our own stories we can portray ourselves with the complexity we deserve as fully realized human beings. We must teach others to see, not just to see our gender, but also our humanity.

EMPOWER

We must empower ourselves

We can build support and family networks that allow us to have a self maintaining community. We can help each other --we can help ourselves! We house, educate, and support one another as we give power to the new generation. Our movement is getting younger and more radical and still we are interfacing with power brokers in our city. We are becoming those power brokers. Our conscience in sharing that power with each other is lethal to the forces that make our lives more difficult.
